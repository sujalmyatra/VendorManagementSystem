5 Entities :
Customer, Vendor, Payment, Invoice, InvoiceList(items with quantity & price)
all have common Id (Base Entity)
Customer & Vendor can be SoftDeleted
Audit Fields (CreatedAt, CreatedBy, UpdatedAt, UpdatedBy) for Customer & Vendors
Payment can be of 5 types (Cash, UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking)
If Payment fails then give another chance. Give Total 6 chances. If Payment fails on 6th chance then generate exception("Payment Failed"), also the payment pattern should be Exponential ( after 1st payment fail wait for 10 seconds, then try again. if again fails then wait for 20 seconds, then again try........ ).
When Order is Created (Payment Done), Generate Event (Order Succeed).
Use SOLID & OOPs
